Phase 1

Discover Your Passion & Find Your Voice

Research the Legislative Landscape in Your State

Confirm Deadlines (drafting & introducing bills, committee sessions, floor votes)

Phase 2

Meet with like-minded groups and share your vision for change (nonprofits, cultural influencers)

Identify Representatives and Senators with Passion for Your Cause and Share Your Personal Story (target judiciary)

Find a Sponsor to Champion Your Bill, ideally one Democrat and one Republican

Phase 3

Create Draft Language for Your Bill

Leverage Media to Help Tell Your Story, Continue to Build Relationships & Find Co-Sponsors

Share Your Personal Story to Help Influence Action by Testifying to Legislators

Phase 4

Committee Debates & Votes on Bill

Target Senate President & House Speaker to Urge Passage of Bill

Senate or House to Vote on Bill on the Floor

Bill Passes Chamber and Advances to Next Chamber

Congratulations! You have done your research, built a strong coalition, shared your story and officially created a law! This is the power of hope! #RiseUp!
